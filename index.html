About me

I started my journey by studying physics at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. Sortly after I pursued a M.Sc. degree in Computationaly Physics, I moved to France where I started my PhD in experimental particle physics at CERN.

After finishing my studies, I started working as a Data Scientist in Startups. Currently I am leading the Data Science team.

For more details you can have a look on my CV.

When I am not working, you will usually find my in the montains or cycling my bike. I love science and arts.
