    About me

    I started my scientific journey studying physics at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. Shortly after I pursued a M.Sc. degree in Computationally Physics, I moved to France where I started my PhD in experimental particle physics at CERN. You can have a look at my PhD thesis here.

    Physics is amazing and I've met the most curious and intelligent people I know in the field... there is something about physicist that I can not explain. But as many of us, I also moved into the world of industry for the benefits of a more "stable" life.

    Even though it may seems like a "monotonous" move, I cannot live without my true nature - my curious mind, by just getting one boring job. My fairy-dust comes in and I am trying to add a research sense to any project I am working on. I can get bored really easily, that is why Data Science was a good switch for me.

    I have a strong acumen for problem solving, and I enjoy challenges. I like simplifying complicated subjects and being able to explain them to audience of different backgrounds.

    When I am not working, you will usually find my hiking or running at the mountains or cycling my bike. I take a great pleasure on challenging myself and I am staying truly disciplined to achieve my goals. I can live without creating art and currently I am into the painting side of it, you can have a look at my paintings here.
